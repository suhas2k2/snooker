Welcome to our snooker club!

We are a friendly and welcoming club that offers a variety of snooker tables for you to enjoy. We also have a bar and a cafe where you can relax and have a drink.

We are open seven days a week, so you can come and play snooker whenever you like. We also offer lessons for beginners, so if you are new to the game, we can help you get started.

Our snooker tables

We have a variety of snooker tables to choose from, including two full-size tables and two smaller tables. We also have a pool table and a darts board.

Our tables are all in excellent condition and are regularly cleaned and maintained.

Our bar and cafe

We have a fully-stocked bar and a cafe where you can relax and have a drink. We also offer a variety of food, including sandwiches, wraps, and pastries.

Our bar and cafe is open seven days a week, so you can come and enjoy a drink or a snack whenever you like.
